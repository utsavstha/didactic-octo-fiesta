Crafting Hospitality service Excellence Since 2023 across Australia

Rising Sun Hospitality was established in 2023 with a mission to deliver high-quality, reliable, and affordable cleaning and labour hire solutions across Sydney. We specialise in a range of services, including commercial cleaning for offices and warehouses, carpet and upholstery cleaning, data centre cleaning, strata cleaning, outsourced hotel services, and labour hire solutions.

Our outsourced hotel services cover a comprehensive range of offerings such as concierge and porter services, guest service agents, stewarding, minibar operations, housekeeping, banquet services, and public area cleaning.

With valuable industry experience as the cornerstone of our success—and that of our clients—we are committed to maintaining the highest safety standards while delivering exceptional service at competitive prices.
